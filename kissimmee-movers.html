FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Do you serve all of Kissimmee?

Yes — every neighborhood. We move families in Celebration, Poinciana, Buenaventura Lakes (BVL), Loma Vista, Storey Lake, Indian Wells, Bellalago, Solivita, Tapestry, Doral Hills, Mill Run, Reedy Creek, and everywhere in between. Same hourly rate, no zone fees, no fuel surcharges.

How much does a Kissimmee move cost?

$75 per mover, per hour, with a 2-hour minimum. A typical 1-bedroom Kissimmee move runs about $300-$500 (2 movers, 2-3 hours). A 2-bedroom move with our truck runs about $700-$900. A 3-bedroom or stairs-up job runs about $1,000-$1,300. Use the calculator above for an instant estimate, or call us for a custom quote.

¿Hablan español? Do you have a bilingual crew?

Sí — tenemos crew bilingüe que atiende a familias hispanas en todo Kissimmee. Hablamos español con respeto y cuidamos sus cosas como si fueran nuestras. Mudanzas honestas, precios claros, sin sorpresas. Llámenos al (321) 758-0094.

Can you handle gated community moves in Kissimmee (Bellalago, Solivita, Storey Lake)?

Yes — gated community moves are some of the most common jobs we do in Kissimmee. We coordinate gate access, visitor passes, and any HOA paperwork (like a Certificate of Insurance) before move day. Just give us the community name and any access details when you book.

What if the move takes longer than I booked?

Easy — we just keep going at $75 per mover per hour. No re-booking, no surprise fees. You only pay for the time we actually work.

What if my move is shorter than 2 hours?

The 2-hour minimum still applies. Most moves take longer than people think, and we want our crew compensated for showing up. If you finish faster, you've still got us on-site for the full window.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed and insured in Florida. We can send you proof of insurance and our license number on request. Just ask.

What if you damage something?

We're insured for it, and our crews are trained to wrap furniture before moving it. In hundreds of moves, we've kept claims to a minimum and resolved them all directly with the customer.

Do you bring tools and supplies?

Yes — dollies, blankets, straps, and basic tools to disassemble and reassemble standard furniture. All free, included in the hourly rate.

Do you do packing and unpacking?

Yes — we can pack a whole house, just the kitchen, or just the fragile stuff. Same $75 per mover, per hour. We bring boxes, packing paper, and tape on request (small materials fee for supplies). Tell us what you need when you book.

Do you do long-distance moves?

Yes — anywhere in Florida and out of state. Same hourly rate for the crew, plus a flat truck fee based on the distance. We give you a transparent custom quote up front so there are no surprises on moving day.

Can you move my storage unit?

Absolutely. Moving in or out of a storage unit is one of the most common jobs we do. Climate-controlled, ground-level, or stairs-up — same hourly rate. Just tell us the unit address and access details when you book.

How do I pay?

A small deposit holds your slot when you book. The balance is paid after the job by card, Cash App, or Zelle. No payment up front for the full amount.
