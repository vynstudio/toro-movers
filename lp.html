Common questions

How much does a move cost?

$75 per mover, per hour, with a 2-hour minimum. A typical 1-bedroom move runs $300-$500. A 2-bedroom with truck runs $700-$900.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed and insured in Florida. We send proof on request.

Do you do packing and long-distance?

Yes — loading, unloading, packing, unpacking, local, long-distance, and storage moves. Same hourly rate.

How do I pay?

A small deposit holds your date ($50 labor-only, $125 with truck). Balance paid after the move by card, Cash App, or Zelle.
