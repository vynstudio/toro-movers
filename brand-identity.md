### Color rules
- **Red is for action** — buttons, price tags, CTAs, the word "MOVERS" in the logo
- **Charcoal is for trust** — headlines, navigation, body text
- **White/Sand for breathing room** — backgrounds, cards
- **Never use red for body text** — only for accents and CTAs
- **Never use more than 2 colors in a single ad creative** — pick Red + Charcoal or Red + White

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Headlines** | System font stack (-apple-system, SF Pro, Segoe UI, Roboto) | 800 (Extra Bold) | Tight letter-spacing (-0.02em), ALL CAPS for brand name only |
| **Body** | Same system stack | 400-500 | Relaxed line-height (1.6), warm and readable |
| **CTAs / Buttons** | Same system stack | 700-800 | Pill-shaped buttons, bold, centered |
| **Eyebrow labels** | Same system stack | 700 | 11px, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em, red pill background |

### Typography rules
- **Headlines should feel bold and direct** — short, punchy, no filler words
- **Body copy should feel warm and honest** — like talking to a neighbor, not a corporation
- **Numbers should be prominent** — $75, $300, 4.9★, 29 reviews — always bold, often in red
- **The brand name "TORO MOVERS" is ALWAYS in all caps**

## Brand Voice

### Personality
| Trait | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Honest** | Say what you mean, no marketing fluff | "The price you book is the price you pay." |
| **Direct** | Lead with the answer, not the setup | "$75 per mover, per hour. 2-hour minimum." |
| **Warm** | Family-business feel, not corporate | "Same crew that quotes your move shows up to do it." |
| **Confident** | Know your worth, no apologies | "Insured, careful, and we've never scratched a piano." |
| **Bilingual** | Weave in Spanish naturally | "Mudanzas honestas. Manos fuertes." |

### Voice do's
- Use contractions (we'll, you're, don't)
- Use second person (you, your)
- Use real numbers ($75, $300, 29 reviews, 2-hour minimum)
- Use short sentences — one idea per sentence
- Name specific neighborhoods (not "the Orlando area")
- Acknowledge the customer's anxiety about moving — then resolve it

### Voice don'ts
- Never say "real human" — say "a member of our team"
- Never promise "if the move takes less time, you pay less" — say "2-hour minimum, then billed by the hour"
- Never use corporate jargon (leverage, synergy, best-in-class)
- Never use exclamation marks in headlines
- Never say "cheap" — say "honest pricing" or "fair rate"
- Never promise specific arrival times — say "same-week scheduling"

## Ad Creative Guidelines

### Image style
- **Real photos only** — no stock photography, no AI-generated images
- **Show the crew working** — loading boxes, wrapping furniture, organizing trucks
- **Show real Central Florida settings** — palm trees, Florida homes, driveways, docks
- **Warm natural lighting** — daylight, golden hour, not studio-lit
- **People should look real** — sweat, tattoos, work clothes, smiles
- **The family portrait** (owner + mother on the dock) is the brand hero image

### Image don'ts
- No generic happy-family-on-couch stock photos
- No clipart or illustrated graphics (except the bull logo)
- No before/after layouts
- No images of empty trucks or empty rooms (show action, not absence)
- No text overlays that cover more than 20% of the image

### Ad format guidelines

**Single image ads:**
- Hero image (crew in action) fills the frame
- Price point in large text at top or bottom ($300 / $875 / $75/hr)
- One-line headline below image
- Brand colors for text overlay (red on white, white on charcoal)

**Carousel ads:**
- Slide 1: Hook/price ($300 — Loading Help)
- Slide 2: What's included (2 movers, 2 hours, dollies, blankets)
- Slide 3: Social proof (4.9★, 29 reviews, real quote)
- Slide 4: CTA (Get a Free Quote / Call now)

**Video ads (15-30 sec):**
- Open with the problem (moving day stress, heavy lifting)
- Show the crew arriving and working (5 seconds)
- Show the price ($75/hr overlay)
- End with logo + phone + CTA

**Stories/Reels ads:**
- Vertical (9:16)
- First 2 seconds: price hook or pain point
- Middle: crew in action
- End: CTA button + phone number
- Keep text minimal — let the visuals work
